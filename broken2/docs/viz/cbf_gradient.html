e_i and L_g h visualization

Left: position space (top-down view). Drag the robot. e_i points away from the obstacle (unit vector). L_g h is along e_i but its magnitude shrinks exponentially with distance.
Right: velocity space (also 2D). Drag the u_des tip. The dashed line is the CBF constraint boundary; the shaded side is unsafe. u_safe is the perpendicular projection of u_des onto the boundary when u_des violates the constraint.
